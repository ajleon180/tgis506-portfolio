Capstone Project

My capstone project for the Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies program at the University of Washington, Tacoma will explore whether remote sensing can be used to predict changes in land use regulations by identifying changes in land cover. The City of Mercer Island will be used as a test case, as regulation changes in Mercer Island have affected how properties within the city have been developed.

About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies at the University of Washington, Tacoma. to advance my understanding of GIS and spatial analysis. I graduated from the University of Washington, Seattle in 2010 and have been working as a municipal land use planner for the past 8 years at Yakima County, WA and the City of Mercer Island, WA. My goal is to apply the knowledge obtained from my master's program to help provide solutions to problems faced by municipal governments.
